# AirVPN Privacy Policy (draft)

Last updated: 2026-07-21

AirVPN provides a VPN client and Telegram-based subscription service.

## Data we process

- Telegram user id and username (bot commerce only)
- Restore codes (stored hashed) for app entitlement
- VPN usage counters from the panel (data used / expiry)
- Payment receipts submitted for plan purchase (KBZPay verification)

## App behavior

- The Android app routes device traffic through AirVPN servers while connected
- Server configs are delivered encrypted from our API; there is no built-in config editor
- Restore codes are stored in encrypted app storage on device

## What we do not do

- We do not sell personal data
- We do not require a username/password login in the app
